# User Manual (Member)

This guide is for customers and instructors.

## 1. Create an account

1. Open the app and click **Register**.
2. Fill in your name, email and password. Pick Customer or Instructor.
3. (Optional) Enter a referral code if a friend gave you one.
4. Click **Register**. You will see "Waiting for approval".

## 2. Log in

- After the admin approves you, log in with your email and password.
- If you are not approved yet, you will see a "Waiting for approval" page.

## 3. Your dashboard

The dashboard shows your membership status, category, expiry date and days left,
plus your reward points.

## 4. Pay for membership

1. Go to **Payments**.
2. Choose a category, payment type (registration or monthly) and number of months.
3. Click **Pay**. Your expiry date updates automatically.

## 5. Book a facility

1. Go to **Facilities**.
2. Facilities your membership includes are marked "Included".
3. Pick one, choose a date and click **Book**. You earn points for booking.

## 6. Referrals and rewards

- **Referrals** page shows your referral code and who you referred.
- **Rewards** page shows your points and lets you withdraw them (there is a
  minimum amount based on your category).

## 7. Notifications

The **Notifications** page shows messages like approval, payment received and
points earned.
